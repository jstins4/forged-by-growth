# Three Mindset Shifts for Leaders

Leadership isn't a title — it's a way of being. Here are three shifts that can transform how you lead.

## 1. From Certainty to Curiosity

The best leaders don't have all the answers. They have the best questions.

## 2. From Control to Trust

Micromanagement signals fear. Trust signals strength.

## 3. From Achievement to Growth

Results matter, but the person you become in pursuit of them matters more.
