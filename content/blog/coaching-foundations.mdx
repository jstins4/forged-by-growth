# The Foundations of Developmental Coaching

Developmental coaching goes beyond traditional coaching by focusing on the whole person — not just behaviors, but the underlying structures of meaning-making that shape how you experience the world.

## Core Principles

- **Meet the client where they are.** Every person has a unique developmental profile.
- **Work with the whole person.** Mind, body, emotions, and relationships are all interconnected.
- **Build new capacities.** Rather than fixing problems, we build the muscles needed for the next level.

The journey is yours. We're just here to help you forge it.
