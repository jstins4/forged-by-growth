# Welcome to Forged by Growth

We believe that growth isn't about fixing what's broken — it's about evolving the architecture of how you move through the world.

## Our Philosophy

Based on Integral Coaching Canada principles, we look at your "way of being" and build new "muscles." The process involves three stages:

1. **The Heat** — The tension between who you are today and the person your future requires you to become.
2. **The Strike** — Targeted practices that challenge your current perspectives and build new capacities.
3. **The Result** — A more complex, capable version of yourself.

## What to Expect

Each coaching engagement is tailored to where you are and where you want to go. Whether you're a leader navigating complexity or an individual ready to evolve, we meet you where you are.
